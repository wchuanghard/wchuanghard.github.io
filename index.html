Hi there, I'm Will Chuang!


A math geek!
Passionate about discovering and collecting unsolved problems, and new points of view of the problems that have been solved, not the least of which is an invariants finder.
Research interests: information geometry, Hodge-de Rham theory, moduli spaces of curves, analytic number theory, intersection numbers, eigenvalue distributions, combinatorics, unsupervised feature learning, human intelligence, and artificial intelligence.
